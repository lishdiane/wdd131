My Health Journal

This name shows the purpose of keeping a personal health journal.

Site Purpose

This site provides a place for people that are interested in their health to keep track of the food they eat and read helpful tips on keeping a healthy lifestyle.

Scenarios

  • What are some tips to maintaning my health?
  • How do I log food into my food journal?
  • What are my macro percentages?
